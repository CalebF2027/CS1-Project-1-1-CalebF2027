Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious Chocolate Chip Cookies!

These cookies are described on allrecipes.com as "truly the best!" They take 20 minutes to prep and 10 minutes to cook. The total time is 30 minutes, and it serves 48 cookies if you follow the recipe correctly.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Nuts (optional)
    • Steps
      1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
      2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
      3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
      4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
      5. Bake until the edges are golden brown.

    Buttermilk Donuts

    Baked Buttermilk Donuts

    These Donuts are described on allrecipes.com as "crispy on the oustide, light and airy in the inside" They take 20 minutes to prep and 25 minutes to cook. The total time is an hour, and it serves 12 donuts if you follow the recipe correctly.

    Ingredients

    • Cooking Spray
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 cup white sugar
    • 2 tablestones cornstarch
    • 1 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1 teaspoon freshly ground nutmeg
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1 cup buttermmilk
    • 2 eggs
    • 1 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 4 tablespoons vegetable shortening, melted and cooled slightly
    • 1 cup sifted powdered sugar (Optional)
    Steps
  • 1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  • 2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  • 3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  • 4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  • 5. Bake until the edges are golden brown.
